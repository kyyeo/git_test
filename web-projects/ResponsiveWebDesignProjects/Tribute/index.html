Lee Kuan Yew

An important Singaporean man

A short time line of Lee Kuan Yew's life:

  • 1923 - Born on 16 September
  • 1935 - Attended Raffles Institution
  • 1942 - World War II paused his education
  • 1946 - Studied law at Cambridge
  • 1951 - Called to Singapore's bar
  • 1954 - People's Action Party was officially inaugurated on 21 November
  • 1955 - Won Tanjong Pagar seat
  • 1959 - PAP wins the general election; Lee is sworn in as Prime Minister on 5 June
  • 1990 - Lee becomes Senior Minister; steps down as Prime Minister, as well as Secretary-General of the PAP
  • 2015 - Passed away on 23 March

"Whoever governs Singapore must have that iron in him. Or give it up. This is not a game of cards. This is your life and mine. I’ve spent a whole lifetime building this and as long as I’m in charge, nobody is going to knock it down."

-- Lee Kuan Yew, Prime Minister of Singapore
